Our history

United Group Healthcare was founded in 1984 with a vision to revolutionize healthcare by providing compassionate, comprehensive, and patient-focused services. Established by a small group of passionate medical professionals, the organization was built on the principles of excellence, accessibility, and innovation. In the 1980s, United Group Healthcare emerged as a pioneer in integrating multidisciplinary services under one roof. Starting with a handful of doctors, the group quickly expanded to include skilled surgeons and experienced therapists, creating a robust network of care for diverse medical needs. By the 1990s, the organization had become a trusted name in the community, known for its quality care and patient-first approach. With advancements in medical technology, the group introduced state-of-the-art surgical procedures and therapeutic practices. In the 2000s, United Group Healthcare launched specialized programs to meet evolving patient needs, from preventive care to rehabilitation. The organization remained committed to delivering exceptional care while adapting to the changing healthcare landscape. Today, United Group Healthcare reflects decades of dedication and progress. With a world-class team of medical professionals, we uphold our founding values of compassion, integrity, and innovation, continuing to prioritize the health and well-being of our community.
